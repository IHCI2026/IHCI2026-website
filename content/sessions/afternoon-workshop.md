A practical workshop where participants will learn to prototype accessible user interfaces
using modern design tools. Bring your laptop and be ready to build!

**What you'll learn:**
- WCAG 2.1 accessibility guidelines
- Designing for screen readers
- Keyboard navigation patterns
- Color contrast and visual design
- Testing with assistive technologies
